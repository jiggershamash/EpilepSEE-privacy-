EpilepSEE Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

1. Who We Are

EpilepSEE is a personal epilepsy tracking application developed and maintained as an independent app. For any privacy related enquiries, contact: epilepseeapp@gmail.com

2. Overview

EpilepSEE is designed with privacy as a core principle. The app operates entirely offline. No personal data is collected, transmitted, or stored by the developer or any third party at any time. All data entered into the app remains exclusively on your device.

3. What Data the App Stores

EpilepSEE allows you to record the following information, all of which is stored locally on your device only:

  • Seizure events including date, time, type, duration, severity, triggers, location, and notes
  • Recovery information including symptoms, duration, and medical assistance details
  • Daily health factors including sleep, stress, medication adherence, mood, and menstrual cycle information
  • Free text notes and observations
  • Emergency contact information you choose to enter

This data constitutes special category health data under UK GDPR. It is stored solely in your device's local storage and is never accessible to the developer or any third party.

4. Lawful Basis for Processing

Under UK GDPR, processing of special category health data requires an explicit lawful basis. EpilepSEE relies on the following:

  • Explicit consent — by choosing to enter health information into the app, you are providing explicit consent for that data to be stored locally on your device for your personal use
  • Legitimate interests — the processing is necessary for the legitimate interest of enabling you to manage and track your own health condition

As the developer does not access, process, or store any of this data, the developer's role as a data controller is limited to providing the tool itself, not the data within it.

5. Special Category Data

Health data, including epilepsy and seizure related information, is classified as special category data under Article 9 of UK GDPR. EpilepSEE treats this data with the highest level of care. As all data remains on your device and is never transmitted externally, the risk of unauthorised access or misuse by the developer is zero. You retain full control over your health data at all times.

6. Data Retention

Data is retained on your device for as long as you choose to keep it. You can delete individual entries or all data at any time from within the app. Uninstalling the app will remove all locally stored data. The developer holds no copies of your data and cannot recover it on your behalf.

7. Your Rights Under UK GDPR

Under UK GDPR you have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • Right of access — all your data is visible within the app at all times
  • Right to erasure — you can delete any or all entries from within the app at any time
  • Right to portability — you can export all your data as a JSON backup file or as CSV and PDF exports from within the app
  • Right to rectification — you can edit any entry from within the app at any time
  • Right to restrict processing — as all processing occurs locally on your device, you control this entirely
  • Right to object — you may stop using the app and delete all data at any time

As the developer does not hold or process your data externally, data subject requests regarding the content of your health records should be addressed by you directly within the app. For any other privacy enquiries contact epilepseeapp@gmail.com

8. Automated Decision Making

EpilepSEE does not make any automated decisions that produce legal or similarly significant effects on users. Chart visualisations and statistical summaries displayed within the app are generated locally from your own data for your personal reference only and do not constitute automated decision making under UK GDPR.

9. Data Sharing

EpilepSEE does not share your data with any third party under any circumstances. No analytics, advertising, or tracking tools are used within the app. No internet connection is required or used during normal operation.

10. Backups and Exports

The app provides backup and export functionality that saves files directly to your device. These files are under your full control. The developer has no access to backup or export files. If you choose to share these files with a healthcare provider or any other party, that sharing is solely your decision and responsibility.

11. Security

Your data is stored in your device's local storage, protected by your device's own security measures including screen lock and device encryption where enabled. The developer recommends keeping your device secured with a PIN, pattern, or biometric lock. The developer cannot be held responsible for unauthorised access resulting from an unsecured device.

12. Children

EpilepSEE is not directed at children under 13. Where the app is used on behalf of a child by a parent or guardian, the parent or guardian is responsible for all data entered and retains full control as all data remains on their device.

13. ICO Registration

As EpilepSEE does not collect, store, or process personal data externally, and all data processing occurs solely on the user's own device, the developer's obligations as a data controller are limited. If you have concerns regarding data protection compliance you may contact the Information Commissioner's Office at ico.org.uk

14. Changes to This Policy

If this privacy policy is updated, the revised version will be published at this URL with an updated date. Continued use of the app following any changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

15. Contact
