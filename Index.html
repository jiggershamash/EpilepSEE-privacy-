EpilepSEE Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

Overview

EpilepSEE is a personal epilepsy tracking app designed for people living with epilepsy. We take your privacy seriously. This policy explains how EpilepSEE handles your data.

Data Collection

EpilepSEE does not collect, transmit, or store any personal data on external servers. All data you enter — including seizure logs, recovery entries, daily factors, notes, and emergency information — is stored exclusively on your device using your browser or app's local storage.

No Internet Connection Required

EpilepSEE operates entirely offline. No data is sent to any server, third party, or cloud service at any time. The app does not require an internet connection to function.

Health Data

EpilepSEE allows you to log sensitive health information including seizure activity, medication adherence, and related medical details. This information never leaves your device. It is not accessible to the developer or any third party.

Backups

EpilepSEE includes a backup feature that exports your data as a JSON file saved directly to your device. This file is under your full control. The developer has no access to backup files.

Exports

PDF and CSV exports are generated on your device and saved locally or shared at your discretion. The developer has no access to exported files.

UK GDPR

As EpilepSEE does not collect, process, or store any personal data externally, the app operates as a purely local tool. No data processing agreements are required as no personal data is handled by the developer.

Children

EpilepSEE is not directed at children under 13. Parents or guardians using the app on behalf of a child retain full control over all data as it remains on their device.

Changes to This Policy

If this privacy policy changes, the updated version will be published at this URL with a revised date.
